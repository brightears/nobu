## Visual Style

**Overall Aesthetic:** Vibrant, modern luxury

### Key Design Elements
- Glassmorphism (backdrop blur, translucent cards)
- Gradient overlays (cyan → teal → lavender)
- Soft shadow effects with color-matched glows
- Rounded corners (generous border radius)
- Smooth hover transitions and animations
- Floating blob backgrounds with blur effects
- High contrast between light and dark sections

### Design Principles
- Clean, minimal layouts
- Generous white space
- Feature cards with hover elevation
- Icon-based feature blocks
- Centered layouts with max-width constraints
- Responsive grid systems

---

## For DJ Presentation

**Recommended approach:**
- Use **Deep Teal** or **Luxury Black** as primary background
- **Brand Cyan** for accent highlights and DJ name headers
- **Off-White** or **Pure White** for text on dark backgrounds
- **Playfair** for DJ names (elegant, sophisticated)
- **Inter** for bio text and details
- Include logo in corner or footer
- Use gradient overlays on DJ photos for visual cohesion
