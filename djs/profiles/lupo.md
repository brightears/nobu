## Bio

Born in Stuttgart to Italian parents, Lupø began his musical journey in Berlin's late-90s underground, managing a Techno club and curating the city's first Hip Hop event inside a techno venue. After stints in Mongolia and Bali where he founded a House event label and held residencies at various locations. In Bangkok, Lupø holds residencies at Tribe Beach Club, Aether, Oskar, Gigi Restaurant, Iron Balls and more venues across the city.
