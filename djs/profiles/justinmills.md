## Bio

Bangkok DJ veteran with 20+ years of experience and a decade-long residency at the legendary Bed Supperclub. Co-founder of DJ/MC collective Bass Clef and creator of the eclectic night "Taboo." Has performed at Wonderfruit festival for three years and held residencies at top Bangkok venues including W Hotel, Maggie Choo's, Havana Social, and Smalls.
