## Bio

Bangkok house music pioneer with over 25 years of experience. Former resident at Ministry of Sound Bangkok for two years, performing alongside world-class DJs including Felix Da Housecat, Judge Jules, Benjamin Diamond, Joey Negro, and Spiller. Released compilation on Voodoo Records (Grammy) featuring Defected label tracks. Co-founder of Ballloom Records.
