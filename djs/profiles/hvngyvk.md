## Bio

Thai DJ and producer with over 10 years of experience in the electronic music scene. 2nd Runner Up at the Pioneer Thailand Lady DJ Championship 2015. Has warmed up for major international acts including Yvesv, Riot Ten, SCNDL, G.T.A, Omnia, Jetfire, Sick Individuals, and Jewelz & Sparks. Performed at 808 Festival 2019 (alongside Calvin Harris, Skrillex, Zedd). Released two original tracks in 2024: "Attention" and "Techtino" on Spotify under the Hwhy.K alias. Extensive residency history across Bangkok's top clubs.
